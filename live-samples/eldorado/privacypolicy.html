Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the El Dorado Winery Assn. website. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages may include:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Credit/Debit Card Information

On ticket orders, you can submit information about other people. For example, if you order tickets online you will need to submit each ticket holder's basic contact information.

The Way We Use Information:

We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We use the information you provide about someone else when placing an order only to list them as a ticket holder and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We use email addresses to answer the email we receive, and send you occasional e-newsletter updates on activities. You may opt out of these newsletter distributions at any time by hitting the "unsubscribe" button on the newsletter. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. You can register with our website if you would like to receive postcard mailings to alert you to our events, or to request a brochure. Information you submit on our website will only be used for this purpose. Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
