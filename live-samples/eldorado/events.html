The Blind Barrel

Postponed to June 2016 - Details to Come

The Gold Trail Grange: 319 State Highway 49 / Marshall Gold Discovery State Historic Park / Coloma, CA

The Blind Barrel is an El Dorado Winery Association event celebrating the repeal of Prohibition with the passage of the Twenty-first Amendment to the United States Constitution on December 5,1933.

Join us in period garb as we serve great local wine at our authentic speakeasy venue—a 1920's grange hall in the center of the historic Marshall Gold Discovery State Park! This intimate venue only holds 250 guests—don't miss your chance to be one of the few to attend the inaugural event! Sure to become a cult classic, the Blind Barrel is one shin-dig you don't want to miss.

Enjoy a gourmet soup-line and wine tasting of the best whites, reds and rose's that El Dorado has to offer. Mix and match bottles from our BLACK MARKET and dance the night away to a rousing swing band.

Prizes will be awarded for best prohibition-era outfit, so get out your best duds, shine your shoes and shake a leg at this exclusive soiree!

A portion of the proceeds will be donated to MORE—Mother Lode Rehabilitiation Enterprises.

Tickets are $50/person (non-refundable)

Passport 2016

Mark your calendar for Passport 2016—coming next April 9,10 & 16,17.

The El Dorado Winery Association invites you to join us for the 25th Annual Passport Event - Taste at a Higher Level! With over 150 years of history steeped in gold and agriculture, the El Dorado region boasts high elevations, unique vineyard soils, and premium wines that exhibit a sense of place. This is your Passport to California's most friendly, intimate, and diverse wine regions - there is something for every wine lover here.

Enjoy wine tasting with our winemakers, sampling perfectly paired foods, and special musical guests all while surrounded by the stunning views of the Sierras. The fun does not stop after one day - instead, this event spans Saturday and Sunday – allowing you to fully experience El Dorado!

Vist www.passporteldorado.com for more information.
