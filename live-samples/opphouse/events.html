GoodSearch.com PSA

  • Feb 21, 2014
  • Giving

What if Opportunity House earned a penny every time you searched the Internet? Or how about if a percentage of every purchase you made online went to support our cause? Well, now it can!

GoodSearch.com is a new Yahoo-powered search engine that donates half its advertising revenue, about a penny per search, to the charities its users designate. Use it just as you would any search engine, get quality search results from Yahoo, and watch the donations add up!

Just go to www.GoodSearch.com and be sure to enter Opportunity House as the charity you want to support. And, be sure to spread the word!
